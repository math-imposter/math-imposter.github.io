Comparison with Conformal Prediction under Ambiguous Ground Truth (Stutz et al., 2023)

Implementation of Stutz et al. Their problem is different than ours, they have uncertainty in ground truth labels, so they have multiple p-values a calibration point and they use ECDF method to combine the p-values. We do their method (Algorithm 2 of their paper) but with m=1.
